## Business Onboarding — 3-Layer Architecture

### Layer 1 — Ecosystem Partners (B2B · High value · Low volume)

| Name | Who | They Get | Families Unlocked |
|------|-----|----------|------------------|
| Mandir Mitra | Temple trusts | Co-branded Prakriti page + 20% ceremony revenue | 500–5,000 |
| Pancha Bhoomi | Gram Panchayats | Village leaderboard + CSR certificate | 200–1,000 |
| Vidya Prakriti | Schools/colleges | Student family tree project | 100–500 |
| Harit Sangh | NGOs/eco-groups | Platform access + data insights | 50–500 |

5 Mandir Mitras = 10,000 families. Zero ad spend.

---

### Layer 2 — Community Champions (B2B2C · Compounding)

| Role | Who | Earns |
|------|-----|-------|
| Prakriti Margdarshak | Pandits (separate track) | ₹300/ceremony + 25% subscription + own Vriksh free |
| Parivar Pramukh | Respected family elder | ₹200/family onboarded + Vriksh free |
| Gram Sevak | Local teacher/influencer | 15% on all area conversions |
| Kshetra Rakshak | District champion | 20% override on downline |

**Pandit Loop:** Ceremony logged → family Prakriti rises → family upgrades → Pandit earns → Pandit logs more ceremonies. Self-reinforcing.
